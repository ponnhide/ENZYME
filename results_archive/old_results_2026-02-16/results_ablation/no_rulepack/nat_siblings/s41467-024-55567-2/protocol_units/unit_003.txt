Basal medium contained Advanced DMEM/F12 (Gibco) supplemented with B‑27 (Gibco), 10 mM HEPES (Corning), 2 mM GlutaMAX (Gibco), Penicillin‑Streptomycin (Gibco), and 1 mM N‑Acetyl‑L‑cysteine (Sigma). For IL‑hSIO culture, 50% Wnt3A conditioned medium, 10% R‑spondin1 conditioned medium, 50 ng/mL EGF (Peprotech), 2 µM DMH1, 0.5 µM A83‑01, 3 µM CHIR, 20 nM TSA, 100 µg/mL pVc (Sigma) and 0.5 µM CP673451 (TargetMol) were added to the basal medium.
