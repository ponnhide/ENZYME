For ER stress induction, ALI-COs at 220 DIV were treated with 50 μM SA (Sigma-Aldrich, 1062771000) or vehicle (equal volume of slice medium). For short-term rescue experiments, slices were either pretreated with 5 μM GSK (R&D Systems, 5107/10) or vehicle overnight in inserts, followed by treatment with GSK or vehicle before SA or vehicle administration.
