Organoid culture medium was prepared by mixing Advanced DMEM/F12 (Gibco) supplemented with B-27 (Gibco), 10 mM HEPES (Corning), 2 mM GlutaMAX (Gibco), Penicillin-Streptomycin (Gibco), and 1 mM N-Acetyl-L-cysteine (Sigma). For IL-hSIO culture, 50% Wnt3A conditioned medium, 10% R-spondin1 conditioned medium, 50 ng/mL EGF (Peprotech), 2 μM DMH1 (ApexBio), 10% R-spondin1 conditioned medium, 10 nM Gastrin I (MCE), 0.5 μM A83-01 (ApexBio), 4 μM CHIR (ApexBio), 20 nM TSA (ApexBio), 100 μg/mL pVc (Sigma) and 0.5 μM CP673451 (TargetMol).
