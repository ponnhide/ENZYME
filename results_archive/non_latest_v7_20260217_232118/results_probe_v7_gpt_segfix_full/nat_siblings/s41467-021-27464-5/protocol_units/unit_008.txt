Images were captured using an Epson Perfection V850 PRO flatbed scanner, or a Leica DMI6000B wide‑field microscope, or a Leica TCS SP8 confocal laser‑scanning microscope, or a Nikon inverted Ti2 microscope equipped with a CSU‑W1 spinning‑disc system. Image acquisition software was Leica LAS X and images were processed using Volocity 6.5.1 software (Quorum Technologies).
