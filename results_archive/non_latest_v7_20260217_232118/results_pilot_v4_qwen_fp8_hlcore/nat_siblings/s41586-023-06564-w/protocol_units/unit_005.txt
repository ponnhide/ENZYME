Three sets of PCR programs were designed to select colonies that showed the insertion of the left, middle and right parts of the donor DNA (PCR1, 5′-TCC TGAGTCCGGACCACTTT and 3′-CACCGCATGTTAGAAGACTTCC; PCR2, 5′-TATGGAGATCCCTCGACCTG and 3′-CCTGGGATACCCCGAAGAGT; PCR3, 5′-TTCTTCTTGATGCTGTGCCG and 3′-GCTCAAGGGGCTTCATGATG).
