Aspirate medium from the base of the transwell and replace with 1 mL per well of cold 4% PFA, also adding one drop of PFA on top of each organoid. Incubate the plate for 30 min at 4 °C... Replace the PBS with 200 µL per well of blocking solution and incubate on a rocking platform at 4 °C for 3–5 h... Add 150 µL of primary antibody solution to each well... Incubate the plate overnight (~16 h) on a rocking platform at 4 °C...
