(B) Mounting of blood vessel organoids ● Timing 2 d
(i) Glue iSpacers onto one 22 × 50-mm coverslip.
(ii) Use a P1000 pipette (cut tip) to pipette organoids into molds, and completely remove the
PBS with a P200 pipette.
(iii) Add drops of mounting medium and distribute the organoids with a P200 pipette tip.
(iv) Carefully lay second 22 × 50-mm coverslip on top, remove excess mounting medium and
dry it in the dark overnight at room temperature.
(v) On the next day, use nail polish for sealing.
