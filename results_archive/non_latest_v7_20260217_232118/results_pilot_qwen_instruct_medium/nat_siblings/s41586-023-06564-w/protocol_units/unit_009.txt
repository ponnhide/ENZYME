On day 6, organoids were transferred to neural medium containing Neurobasal A (Thermo Fisher, catalogue no. 10888022), B-27 supplement without vitamin A (Life Technologies, catalogue no. 12587), GlutaMax (1:100, Life Technologies, catalogue no. 35050061) and penicillin-streptomycin (1:100, Thermo Fisher, catalogue no. 15140163). Neural medium was supplemented with 20 ng ml−1 EGF (R&D Systems) and 20 ng ml−1 FGF2 (R&D Systems) from day 6 to 24 with daily medium change in the first 10 days, and every other day for the subsequent 9 days.
