Resuspend in ~200 µL of HBSS/BSA/glucose.
Add the master mix into the tube of resuspended cells.
Incubate for 15 min on ice.
Wash with 5 mL of HBSS/BSA/glucose and mix.
Spin down for 5 min at 300g, 4 °C.
Discard supernatant.
Resuspend cells.
Obtain one FACS tube and a 70 µm filter.
With the filter over the FACS tube, dispense the resuspended cells through the filter and into the FACS tube.
Seal the tube and place on ice.
